5 CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE

CHARACTERS OF NOLI ME TANGERE

CRISOTOMO IBARRA he son of the most wealthy man in that area, who had returned to the Philippines after 7 years from Europe. His second motherland is Spain and he has the appearance of a Spaniard.
PADRE DAMASO The Fransican priest who framed the father of Crisostomo Ibarra and this man is the villain or antagonist of the story. Throughout the story this man committed several serious crimes and is always arguing with someone. He was stationed in San Diego for 20 years before being forced to move to another place because of committing a crime.
KAPITAN TIAGO He is a very generous and giving man who's palm is open to the whole community and he is a very close friend of Ibarra and he was even kind enough to throw an entire gathering at his home to welcome Ibarra from his trip to Europe.
DON RAFAEL IBARRA This is the father of Crisostomo who is the wealthiest man in that community and because of his wealth many Spaniards and Priests are jelous of that wealth. This led to him being framed for killing a man and he ended up being imprisoned and dying there.
LIEUTENANT The man who was at the gathering and was talking with Crisostomo and Padre Damaso and Sibyla. He was a close friend of Don Rafael and was with him at his last breathe. He was the one to explain to Crisostomo what happened to his father and how he died because no one told him what happened to his father.
